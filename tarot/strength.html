Major Arcana • Card 8

Strength

🔥 Fire ☆ Leo

The infinite patience that gentles the wild, proving that the softest hand holds the strongest power.

▲ Upright Meaning

True power flows from gentleness. Tame your inner beasts not with force but with love. Your quiet courage moves mountains that brute strength cannot.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Self-doubt undermines your natural power. Fear of your own strength keeps you small. Embrace the fierce compassion that lives within you.

Keywords

courage inner strength compassion patience influence

Element: Fire
