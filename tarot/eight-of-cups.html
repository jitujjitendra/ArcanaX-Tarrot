Minor Arcana - Cups • Card 8

Eight of Cups

💧 Water

The sacred walk into the unknown, where leaving behind becomes the bravest act of self-love.

▲ Upright Meaning

What once fulfilled you no longer does. Walk away from the incomplete tower of cups to seek something more real. This departure is not failure - it is evolution.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Fear of leaving the familiar keeps you stuck. Returning to what you abandoned, or inability to move on. Ask honestly: is this contentment or just comfort?
