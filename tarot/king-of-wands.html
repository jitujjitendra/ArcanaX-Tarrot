Minor Arcana - Wands • Card 14

King of Wands

🔥 Fire

The master of flame who builds empires from sparks, where visionary boldness meets the patience to let great things grow.

▲ Upright Meaning

Lead with the fire of vision and the wisdom to sustain it. Your charismatic boldness inspires others to follow. Think big, act decisively, and trust your ability to turn vision into empire.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Impulsiveness or tyrannical leadership. Vision without patience or charisma without substance. The greatest fire is useless if it burns those it should warm.
