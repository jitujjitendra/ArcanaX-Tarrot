Nine of Wands

🔥 Fire

The wounded warrior who finds one more flame within when all others have gone dark, where resilience becomes its own reward.

▲ Upright Meaning

Battered but unbroken, you stand your ground one final time. The end of this trial is closer than exhaustion suggests. Draw on reserves you did not know you had - one more push.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Paranoia or inability to lower defenses. Battle fatigue that distorts perception of threat. Not everything coming toward you is an enemy. Rest before your final stand.

Keywords

resilience persistence last stand courage boundaries

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
