Major Arcana • Card 18

The Moon

💧 Water ☆ Pisces

The cosmic mirror that reflects our deepest fears as shadows on the cave wall, where illusion and intuition dance as one.

▲ Upright Meaning

Not all is as it appears. Navigate by feeling, not sight. The subconscious speaks in symbols and dreams - listen, but do not mistake shadows for substance.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Confusion lifts as truth surfaces. Fears faced lose their distorted power. Trust what emerges from the deep but verify with waking eyes.

Keywords

illusion intuition fear subconscious dreams

Element: Water

Emotions, intuition, relationships, and flow
