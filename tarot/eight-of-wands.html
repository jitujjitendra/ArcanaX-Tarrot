Eight of Wands

🔥 Fire

The moment when all arrows fly true and the universe removes every obstacle between intention and manifestation.

▲ Upright Meaning

Everything accelerates. Messages fly, plans launch, and momentum carries you forward with exhilarating speed. Remove obstacles to flow and let the arrows of intention find their marks.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Delays, frustration, and scattered energy. Arrows launched without aim land nowhere useful. Patience - the speed will return when alignment is restored.

Keywords

speed movement swift action air travel momentum

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
