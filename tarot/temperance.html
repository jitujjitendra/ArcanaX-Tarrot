Major Arcana • Card 14

Temperance

🔥 Fire ☆ Sagittarius

The cosmic alchemist who pours fire into water and creates steam - the art of sacred balance in motion.

▲ Upright Meaning

Blend opposites into gold. Patient mixing of disparate elements creates something neither could be alone. Flow between extremes with the grace of water finding its level.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Imbalance and excess disrupt your harmony. Impatience spoils what needs slow brewing. Return to center and trust the process of gradual integration.

Keywords

balance moderation patience harmony alchemy

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
