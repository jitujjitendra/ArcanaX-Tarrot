Minor Arcana - Swords • Card 14

King of Swords

💨 Air

The sovereign mind whose authority comes not from force but from the undeniable weight of clear truth.

▲ Upright Meaning

Command through clarity of thought and ethical judgment. Your word is law because it is true, not because it is loud. Lead with the mind that sees all angles and chooses wisely.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Tyranny of intellect or cold manipulation. Power without mercy or judgment without compassion. The sharpest mind is useless without the heart to guide it.

Keywords

intellectual authority truth clear judgment ethics analytical power

Element: Air

Intellect, communication, truth, and clarity
