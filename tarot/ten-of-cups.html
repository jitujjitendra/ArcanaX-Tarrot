Minor Arcana - Cups • Card 10

Ten of Cups

💧 Water

The complete rainbow of human emotional experience, where home is not a place but a state of the heart.

▲ Upright Meaning

The rainbow arcs over your emotional home. Deep fulfillment in love, family, and belonging. This is not perfection - it is wholeness, with all its beautiful imperfection.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Broken harmony in family or relationships. The ideal image cracks to reveal reality underneath. True belonging requires accepting imperfection in those we love.

Keywords

emotional fulfillment family harmony divine love bliss homecoming

Element: Water

Emotions, intuition, relationships, and flow
