Minor Arcana - Swords • Card 8

Eight of Swords

💨 Air

The illusion of imprisonment where every bar is a belief and the door was never locked.

▲ Upright Meaning

You feel trapped, but look closely - the bindings are loose and the swords form a cage with gaps. Your prison is mental, built from beliefs about your own powerlessness. Open your eyes.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Breaking free from self-imposed limitations. Realizing your power was never truly taken. The cage dissolves when you stop believing in walls that were never there.

Keywords

restriction imprisonment powerlessness self-limiting beliefs victim mentality

Element: Air

Intellect, communication, truth, and clarity
