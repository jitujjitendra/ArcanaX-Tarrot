Minor Arcana - Swords • Card 9

Nine of Swords

💨 Air

The long dark night of the mind where thoughts become blades and worry becomes its own wound.

▲ Upright Meaning

The darkest hour of the mind, where fear multiplies in the silence of 3 AM. These swords are thoughts, not truths. What haunts you in darkness often dissolves in the light of day and action.

▼ Reversed Meaning

The worst anxiety begins to ease. Reaching out for help with mental burden. Learning that shared fear loses half its weight. Dawn is closer than you think.

Keywords

anxiety nightmares despair mental anguish worry

Element: Air

Intellect, communication, truth, and clarity
