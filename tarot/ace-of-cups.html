Minor Arcana - Cups • Card 1

Ace of Cups

💧 Water

The first drop that becomes the ocean, where every love story begins with opening.

▲ Upright Meaning

A new wellspring of emotion opens within you. Love in its purest form arrives - be it romantic, platonic, or self-love. Open your heart to receive.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Emotional blockage or repressed feelings. The cup is offered but you turn away. Examine what fears prevent you from receiving love.

Keywords

new love emotional awakening intuition compassion creativity

Element: Water

Emotions, intuition, relationships, and flow
