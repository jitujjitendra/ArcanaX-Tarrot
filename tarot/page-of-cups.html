💧 Water

The young mystic who finds a fish in their cup and greets the impossible with wonder rather than fear.

▲ Upright Meaning

A message from the emotional depths arrives unexpectedly. Stay open to creative surprises and intuitive nudges. Approach feelings with childlike curiosity rather than adult judgment.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Emotional immaturity or creative blocks. Ignoring intuitive messages or drowning in sensitivity. Ground your feelings before they sweep you into fantasy.

Keywords

creative inspiration intuitive messages curiosity sensitivity new feelings

Element: Water

Emotions, intuition, relationships, and flow
