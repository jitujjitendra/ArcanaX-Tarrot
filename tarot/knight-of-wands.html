Minor Arcana - Wands • Card 12

Knight of Wands

🔥 Fire

The blazing comet that tears across the sky, proving that audacity and passion can bend the arc of fate.

▲ Upright Meaning

Charge forward with passionate intensity. Your fearless energy attracts adventure and opportunity. Move boldly but not blindly - the fastest horse still needs a rider with vision.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Reckless action or scattered energy. Passion without direction burns everything including yourself. Aim your fire before releasing it on the world.

Keywords

adventure energy passion impulsiveness action

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
