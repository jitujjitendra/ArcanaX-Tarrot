Minor Arcana - Pentacles • Card 7

Seven of Pentacles

🌍 Earth

The patient farmer who trusts the seasons, knowing that watching the pot never makes it boil faster.

▲ Upright Meaning

Pause to assess what your efforts have grown. Not all fruit ripens at the same pace. Patience with your harvest - what seems slow is simply taking the time needed to bear full fruit.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Impatience with results or wasted investment. Effort without strategy yields diminishing returns. Reassess whether this garden is worth continued tending.

Keywords

patience assessment investment perseverance long-term vision

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
