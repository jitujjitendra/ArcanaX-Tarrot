The Chariot

💧 Water ☆ Cancer

The cosmic warrior whose victory comes not from destroying opposition but from mastering the forces within.

▲ Upright Meaning

Victory through sheer force of will. Harness opposing forces and drive forward with unwavering determination. The stars align for those who refuse to stop.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Loss of direction and scattered willpower. Aggression without strategy leads nowhere. Regain control of your inner forces before charging ahead.

Keywords

determination triumph willpower control momentum

Element: Water

Emotions, intuition, relationships, and flow

Zodiac/Planet: Cancer
