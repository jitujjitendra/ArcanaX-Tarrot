Ten of Wands

🔥 Fire

The fire that tries to burn in ten directions at once and learns that focus, not spread, creates true warmth.

▲ Upright Meaning

You carry more than any one person should. Success has become its own burden. Delegate, release, or restructure before the weight of all these wands crushes the fire that lit them.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Learning to delegate or releasing unnecessary burdens. The collapse of over-commitment leads to wiser distribution. Put down what is not yours to carry.

Keywords

burden responsibility hard work overwhelm carrying too much

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
