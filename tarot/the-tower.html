Major Arcana • Card 16

The Tower

🔥 Fire ☆ Mars

The cosmic lightning that illuminates by destroying, proving that some truths can only be revealed through the shattering of comfortable lies.

▲ Upright Meaning

Lightning strikes the structures built on false foundations. What crumbles was never truly stable. In the rubble lies liberation - the truth that sets you painfully, beautifully free.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Avoiding inevitable collapse only delays and amplifies it. Internal upheaval that has not yet externalized. Prepare for necessary destruction with grace.

Keywords

upheaval sudden change revelation awakening liberation

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
