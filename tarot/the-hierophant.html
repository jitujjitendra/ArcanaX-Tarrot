Major Arcana • Card 5

The Hierophant

🌍 Earth ☆ Taurus

The bridge between divine wisdom and earthly understanding, channeling ancient truth through living tradition.

▲ Upright Meaning

Seek wisdom from those who walked before you. Tradition carries power when understood deeply. A teacher or system offers the structure your spirit needs now.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Question inherited beliefs that no longer serve your truth. Blind conformity dims your inner light. Find your own path to the sacred.

Keywords

tradition spiritual wisdom conformity mentorship ceremony

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
