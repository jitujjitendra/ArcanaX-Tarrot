Major Arcana • Card 13

Death

💧 Water ☆ Scorpio

The cosmic threshold where form dissolves back into potential, proving that death is merely transformation wearing a mask.

▲ Upright Meaning

Something must end for the new to be born. Release what has completed its cycle with gratitude. This death is not destruction - it is the composting that feeds new life.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Resistance to necessary endings prolongs suffering. Clinging to the dead past prevents rebirth. Let go - you cannot take the old self into the new world.
