Minor Arcana - Swords • Card 5

Five of Swords

💨 Air

The bitter taste of a victory that cost more than defeat ever would, where winning becomes its own punishment.

▲ Upright Meaning

A hollow victory leaves more enemies than allies. Winning this battle may lose the war. Consider whether the prize justifies the bridges burned to claim it.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Moving past conflict and choosing peace over being right. Learning from defeat without bitterness. Sometimes the wisest sword is the one laid down.

Keywords

conflict defeat winning at a cost hostility betrayal

Element: Air

Intellect, communication, truth, and clarity
