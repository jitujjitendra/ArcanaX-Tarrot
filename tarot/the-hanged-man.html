The Hanged Man

💧 Water ☆ Neptune

The cosmic pause where gravity reverses and surrender becomes the most powerful act of will.

▲ Upright Meaning

Suspend your struggle. The answer comes not from pushing harder but from releasing entirely. See the world from a new angle - what seemed stuck is actually transforming.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Needless martyrdom and stalling. You mistake paralysis for patience. The time for waiting has passed - act from your new understanding.

Keywords

surrender new perspective letting go sacrifice patience

Element: Water
