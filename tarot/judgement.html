Major Arcana • Card 20

Judgement

🔥 Fire ☆ Pluto

The cosmic alarm clock that wakes the soul from its earthly sleep, calling all fragments home to wholeness.

▲ Upright Meaning

The trumpet sounds for your awakening. Answer your highest calling without apology. Past selves dissolve as you rise into who you were always meant to become.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Self-judgment blocks your resurrection. Ignoring the call does not silence it. Forgive yourself and answer - the world needs what only you can offer.
