Two of Wands

🔥 Fire

The visionary at the crossroads holding the whole world like a promise, where planning becomes the bridge between dream and destiny.

▲ Upright Meaning

The world lies before you as you hold your vision in one hand and possibility in the other. Plan your expansion with bold ambition. The map is drawn - now choose your direction.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Fear of the unknown prevents expansion. Over-planning replaces action. The world shrinks when you refuse to step beyond familiar walls.

Keywords

planning future vision decisions discovery progress

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
