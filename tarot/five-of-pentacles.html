Minor Arcana - Pentacles • Card 5

Five of Pentacles

🌍 Earth

The necessary winter that teaches us warmth is a gift, and that asking for help is its own form of strength.

▲ Upright Meaning

Material struggle or feeling left out in the cold. Help exists but pride or shame may blind you to it. The stained glass window glows warm - look up and walk toward the door.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Recovery from financial hardship begins. Accepting help after struggling alone. The worst has passed and rebuilding can begin in earnest.

Keywords

hardship loss isolation worry poverty

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
