Minor Arcana - Swords • Card 7

Seven of Swords

💨 Air

The trickster who steals from himself without knowing it, where cleverness without integrity becomes its own trap.

▲ Upright Meaning

Strategy or subterfuge - the line is thin. Sometimes unconventional approaches are necessary, but examine your motives carefully. Are you being clever or just avoiding honest confrontation?

▼ Reversed Meaning

Deception exposed or conscience catching up. Getting caught in your own web. Choose transparency over cleverness - the truth you avoid will find you regardless.

Keywords

deception strategy stealth cunning self-deception

Element: Air

Intellect, communication, truth, and clarity
