Seven of Cups

💧 Water

The hall of mirrors where desire multiplies into infinity, and choosing becomes the only path to having.

▲ Upright Meaning

Many visions shimmer before you, but not all are real. Distinguish true desire from escapist fantasy. Choose one cup and pour your whole self into it.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Clarity emerges from the fog of too many options. Grounding fantasies into actionable reality. Choose and commit rather than forever dreaming.

fantasy choices illusion imagination wishful thinking

Element: Water

Emotions, intuition, relationships, and flow
