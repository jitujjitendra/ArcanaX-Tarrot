Minor Arcana - Pentacles • Card 4

Four of Pentacles

🌍 Earth

The guardian who must learn that true wealth flows and what is clutched too tightly turns to dust.

▲ Upright Meaning

Hold what you have built with appropriate care, but not with a death grip. Security comes from wise stewardship, not fearful hoarding. Know the difference between protecting and imprisoning.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Letting go of material attachment or financial fear. Generosity after a period of holding too tight. Release control to allow flow.

Keywords

security possessiveness control stability conservatism

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
