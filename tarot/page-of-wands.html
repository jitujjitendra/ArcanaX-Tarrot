Page of Wands

🔥 Fire

The young flame that dances with every breeze, where curiosity becomes the compass and enthusiasm the fuel.

▲ Upright Meaning

A new passion ignites with youthful enthusiasm. Explore without needing to know the destination. This creative excitement is meant to be followed, not analyzed.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Lack of direction or commitment phobia. Excitement without follow-through burns bright but brief. Channel your spark before it scatters into smoke.

Keywords

exploration excitement new passion free spirit discovery

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
