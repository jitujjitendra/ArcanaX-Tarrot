Minor Arcana - Swords • Card 11

Page of Swords

💨 Air

The young wind that cuts in every direction at once, learning that precision matters more than speed.

▲ Upright Meaning

A sharp young mind eager to cut through pretense. Approach challenges with intellectual curiosity and quick thinking. Speak truth clearly, but learn when silence is sharper than words.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Gossip, scattered thinking, or all talk without action. Mental energy without direction cuts carelessly. Focus your blade before swinging.

Keywords

curiosity mental agility new ideas communication vigilance

Element: Air

Intellect, communication, truth, and clarity
