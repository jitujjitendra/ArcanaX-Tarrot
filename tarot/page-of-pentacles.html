Minor Arcana - Pentacles • Card 11

Page of Pentacles

🌍 Earth

The eager student who holds the golden coin of possibility, examining it from every angle before investing.

▲ Upright Meaning

A new practical venture or course of study calls to you. Approach material goals with fresh enthusiasm and willingness to learn. Plant seeds of future abundance with youthful dedication.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Lack of focus or impractical dreams. Procrastination on material goals. The plan needs grounding before it can grow into something real.

Keywords

ambition study new venture practicality opportunity

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
