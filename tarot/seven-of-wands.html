Seven of Wands

🔥 Fire

The lone flame on the hilltop that refuses to be extinguished by the winds of opposition.

▲ Upright Meaning

Hold your high ground against those who challenge your position. You earned this elevation - defend it with the fire of conviction. Not everyone who reaches for your crown deserves it.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Overwhelmed by opposition or giving up ground unnecessarily. Exhaustion from constant defense. Sometimes retreat is strategic, not cowardly.

Keywords

defiance courage perseverance standing ground challenge

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
