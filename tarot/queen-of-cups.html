Minor Arcana - Cups • Card 13

Queen of Cups

💧 Water

The deep ocean that holds all feeling without being moved from center, where empathy becomes wisdom.

▲ Upright Meaning

You hold the cup of empathy without drowning in it. Nurture others from your overflow, not your reserves. Your emotional wisdom guides like moonlight on water.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Emotional overwhelm or codependency. Absorbing others feelings as your own. Seal your cup before pouring for others - compassion requires boundaries.

Keywords

emotional intelligence compassion intuition nurturing psychic ability

Element: Water

Emotions, intuition, relationships, and flow
