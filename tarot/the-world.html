Major Arcana • Card 21

The World

🌍 Earth ☆ Saturn

The cosmic dancer at the center of everything, where ending and beginning lose all meaning in the eternal spiral of becoming.

▲ Upright Meaning

The dance of completion spins you toward a new beginning. All lessons integrate into wisdom. You are whole, you are here, and the cosmos celebrates your journey.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Almost complete but something remains unfinished. A lesson not yet integrated delays your graduation. Close the final loop before beginning anew.

Keywords

completion integration accomplishment wholeness cosmic consciousness

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
