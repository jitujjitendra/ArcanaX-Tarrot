Minor Arcana - Swords • Card 12

Knight of Swords

💨 Air

The storm on horseback who cuts through hesitation like wind through fog, where speed becomes destiny.

▲ Upright Meaning

Charge forward with intellectual conviction and unstoppable momentum. Your mind moves faster than most can follow. Channel this fierce mental energy toward worthy targets with precision.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Reckless thinking or hasty action without foresight. Speed without direction is just chaos. Slow your charge long enough to aim properly.

Keywords

ambition action speed determination intellectual pursuit

Element: Air

Intellect, communication, truth, and clarity
