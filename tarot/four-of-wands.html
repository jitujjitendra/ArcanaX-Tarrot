Minor Arcana - Wands • Card 4

Four of Wands

🔥 Fire

The harvest festival where community gathers to honor what grows when people build together with shared fire.

▲ Upright Meaning

A milestone achieved deserves its celebration. Community gathers in joy around what you have built. This is not the end but a beautiful waypoint - honor it fully.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Delayed celebration or instability within community. The party lacks genuine joy. Ensure the foundation supports the revelry before dancing on it.

Keywords

celebration homecoming harmony community milestone

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
