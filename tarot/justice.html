Major Arcana • Card 11

Justice

💨 Air ☆ Libra

The cosmic scales that weigh not just action but intention, where truth is the only currency that never devalues.

▲ Upright Meaning

Truth cuts through illusion. Face consequences with dignity and make decisions with clear-eyed fairness. The universe balances all accounts in time.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Injustice or dishonesty clouds the situation. Accountability avoided today becomes a heavier debt tomorrow. Seek truth even when it is uncomfortable.

Keywords

fairness truth cause and effect clarity accountability

Element: Air

Intellect, communication, truth, and clarity
