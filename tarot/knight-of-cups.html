Minor Arcana - Cups • Card 12

Knight of Cups

💧 Water

The poet-warrior who rides toward love with open visor, where vulnerability becomes the sharpest blade.

▲ Upright Meaning

Follow your heart with graceful pursuit. The romantic quest calls you forward. Lead with charm and emotional intelligence, but ensure your vision matches reality.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Moodiness or unrealistic romantic ideals. Charm without substance or following feelings without discernment. Ground your quest in truth, not just beauty.

Keywords

romance charm imagination following the heart idealism

Element: Water

Emotions, intuition, relationships, and flow
