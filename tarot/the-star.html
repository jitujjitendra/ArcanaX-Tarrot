Major Arcana • Card 17

The Star

💨 Air ☆ Aquarius

The cosmic wellspring that pours infinite light into finite form, proving that hope is the most radical act of creation.

▲ Upright Meaning

After the storm, stars emerge. Pour your healing into the world without fear of emptying - you are connected to an infinite source. Hope is not naive; it is revolutionary.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Disconnection from hope and inspiration. Faith tested by darkness. Remember - stars shine brightest when the night is deepest. Reconnect to your cosmic source.

hope inspiration renewal serenity cosmic connection

Element: Air
