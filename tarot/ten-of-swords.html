Minor Arcana - Swords • Card 10

Ten of Swords

💨 Air

The paradox of total defeat becoming total freedom, where hitting bottom becomes the foundation for rising.

▲ Upright Meaning

The final sword falls and the worst is over. This absolute ending is also the absolute beginning. You cannot be stabbed again - there is nowhere to go but up. Dawn breaks on the horizon.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Refusing to accept an ending or surviving what should have destroyed you. Rising from rock bottom with hard-won wisdom. The worst is truly behind you now.

Keywords

rock bottom ending betrayal defeat new dawn

Element: Air

Intellect, communication, truth, and clarity
