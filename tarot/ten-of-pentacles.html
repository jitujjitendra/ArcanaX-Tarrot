Minor Arcana - Pentacles • Card 10

Ten of Pentacles

🌍 Earth

The ancient tree whose roots and branches connect generations, where true wealth is measured in what outlives us.

▲ Upright Meaning

Generational abundance and lasting legacy. What you build now echoes through time. Consider what you leave behind - true wealth is what endures beyond a single lifetime.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Family financial disputes or lost inheritance. Legacy crumbling or wealth without purpose. Material success means nothing if the foundation of values is hollow.

Keywords

legacy wealth inheritance family establishment

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
