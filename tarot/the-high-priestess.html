The High Priestess

💧 Water ☆ Moon

Guardian of the cosmic threshold, keeper of mysteries that reveal themselves only to those who listen in silence.

▲ Upright Meaning

The veil between worlds grows thin. Trust your inner knowing - the answers you seek lie not in the external world but within the depths of your own soul.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Disconnection from intuition. Secrets kept too long become poison. Allow hidden knowledge to surface and trust what emerges.

intuition mystery inner wisdom subconscious patience

Element: Water
