Minor Arcana - Pentacles • Card 12

Knight of Pentacles

🌍 Earth

The steadfast rider whose tortoise pace builds empires that the hare can only dream of.

▲ Upright Meaning

Steady progress through unwavering commitment. Not the fastest knight, but the most dependable. Your methodical approach builds what flashier efforts cannot sustain.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Stubbornness disguised as dedication, or laziness masked as patience. Stagnation through excessive caution. Sometimes the slow road is just the wrong road.

Keywords

reliability hard work routine patience thoroughness

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
