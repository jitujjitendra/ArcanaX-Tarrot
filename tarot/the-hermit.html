The Hermit

🌍 Earth ☆ Virgo

The lone star that guides by shining inward first, proving that the deepest journeys require no distance at all.

▲ Upright Meaning

Withdraw from the noise to find your signal. The lantern of truth lights only for those willing to walk alone. Solitude is not loneliness - it is communion with self.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Isolation becomes avoidance. Too long in the cave dims your connection to life. Bring your inner wisdom back to the world that needs it.
