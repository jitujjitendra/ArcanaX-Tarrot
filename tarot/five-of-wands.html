Five of Wands

🔥 Fire

The creative crucible where friction between forces generates the heat needed to forge something new.

▲ Upright Meaning

Creative friction sparks growth when channeled well. Competition sharpens your edge if you fight fair. Not all conflict is destructive - some fires forge rather than consume.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Avoiding necessary confrontation or exhaustion from constant battling. End the fight or commit fully - half-hearted conflict helps no one.

Keywords

competition conflict disagreement tension challenge

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
