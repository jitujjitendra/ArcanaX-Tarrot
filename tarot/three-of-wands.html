Three of Wands

🔥 Fire

The watcher on the cliff whose patience is rewarded by sails appearing on the horizon of their own making.

▲ Upright Meaning

Your ships have launched and the horizon beckons. Expansion is underway - trust what you set in motion. The seeds of past planning now sail toward distant shores of possibility.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Delays in expected progress or fear of expansion. Ships sent but not yet returned. Patience with your ventures - not all winds blow on your schedule.

Keywords

expansion foresight overseas opportunities progress exploration

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
