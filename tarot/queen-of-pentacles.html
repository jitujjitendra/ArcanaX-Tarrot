Minor Arcana - Pentacles • Card 13

Queen of Pentacles

🌍 Earth

The earth mother whose practical magic turns houses into homes and resources into nourishment for all.

▲ Upright Meaning

Create abundance that nurtures all who enter your realm. Practical wisdom and generous spirit combine to build a life of sustainable comfort. You make prosperity feel like home.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Work-life imbalance or neglecting practical needs for others. Financial anxiety despite abundance. Nurture yourself with the same generosity you offer others.

Keywords

nurturing abundance practicality comfort financial security generosity

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
