The Lovers

💨 Air ☆ Gemini

The sacred geometry of two souls recognizing themselves in each other, where choice becomes destiny.

▲ Upright Meaning

A profound choice stands before you - one that aligns head and heart. True union begins with loving yourself wholly. Choose what makes your soul sing.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Disharmony and misaligned values. A choice avoided grows heavier with time. Face the truth of what your heart truly desires.
