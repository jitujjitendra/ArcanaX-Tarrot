Major Arcana • Card 19

The Sun

🔥 Fire ☆ Sun

The cosmic fire that asks nothing in return for its warmth, proving that true power lies in effortless radiance.

▲ Upright Meaning

Radiant success illuminates your path. Bask in earned joy without guilt. Your authentic self shines brightest now - let warmth flow outward to all you touch.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Temporary clouds dim but cannot extinguish your light. Delayed success or dimmed enthusiasm. The sun has not gone - only your view is obscured.

Keywords

joy success vitality clarity celebration

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
