Minor Arcana - Wands • Card 6

Six of Wands

🔥 Fire

The triumphant return where the fire of achievement is witnessed and honored by those who understand its cost.

▲ Upright Meaning

Victory rides in and the crowd acknowledges your achievement. Accept recognition with grace - you earned this moment. Let success fuel further ambition rather than breed complacency.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Private success without public recognition, or ego inflated by hollow praise. True victory needs no audience. Let your work speak louder than applause.

Keywords

victory recognition success triumph public acclaim

Element: Fire

Passion, energy, creativity, and transformation
