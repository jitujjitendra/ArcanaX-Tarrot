Minor Arcana - Pentacles • Card 6

Six of Pentacles

🌍 Earth

The scales of giving where true generosity empowers rather than diminishes, and receiving is also an act of grace.

▲ Upright Meaning

Abundance shared multiplies. Whether giving or receiving, do so with grace and without power imbalance. Generosity that empowers rather than creates dependency is true wealth.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Strings attached to generosity or unfair distribution. One-sided giving that creates imbalance. Examine the power dynamics within your exchanges.

Keywords

generosity charity giving and receiving balance sharing

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
