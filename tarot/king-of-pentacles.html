Minor Arcana - Pentacles • Card 14

King of Pentacles

🌍 Earth

The master builder whose wealth serves rather than rules, proving that true abundance is measured in what you create for others.

▲ Upright Meaning

Material mastery through disciplined vision. You have built your kingdom coin by coin with patience and wisdom. Lead your material realm with generosity and grounded authority.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Greed or materialism without soul. Business success at the cost of everything else. A kingdom of gold is still empty without warmth within its walls.

Keywords

wealth business mastery discipline abundance reliability

Element: Earth

Material world, stability, health, and growth
