Minor Arcana - Swords • Card 4

Four of Swords

💨 Air

The sacred rest between battles where the mind heals in the temple of voluntary stillness.

▲ Upright Meaning

The warrior must rest to fight again. Mental exhaustion demands a sacred pause. Retreat is not defeat - it is strategic restoration. Let your mind lie still in the temple of silence.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Restlessness or forced return before recovery is complete. Burnout ignored becomes breakdown. Honor your need for mental rest even when the world demands action.

Keywords

rest recovery contemplation solitude restoration

Element: Air

Intellect, communication, truth, and clarity
