The Fool

💨 Air ☆ Uranus

The eternal wanderer at the cliff of infinite possibility, where every ending births a new beginning.

▲ Upright Meaning

A new chapter unfolds before you. Step forward with childlike wonder and trust the cosmic current carrying you toward your destiny.

▼ Reversed Meaning

Recklessness clouds your judgment. Pause before the edge - not every leap leads to flight. Ground yourself before moving forward.
